Gym Workout Exercises


Exercise 1: Bench Press

Targeted Muscle Group: Chest

Instructions: Lie flat on your back, grip the barbell, and lower it to your chest, then push it back up.

Exercise 2: Squats

Targeted Muscle Group: Legs

Instructions: Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart, lower your body by bending your knees, and then stand back up.

Exercise 3: Pull-Ups

Targeted Muscle Group: Back

Instructions: Hang from a pull-up bar, then pull your body up until your chin is above the bar.

Exercise 4: Plank

Targeted Muscle Group: Core

Instructions: Hold a push-up position with your arms straight and your body in a straight line from head to heels.
